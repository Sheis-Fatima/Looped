# Looped
# What the Product Does
Looped is a student-focused social platform that keeps students informed and up-to-date. It brings together everything that matters on campus, like verified academic updates, peer connections, and events, into a trusted, easy-to-use space.
With Looped, students can finally stop juggling multiple WhatsApp groups and channels.
# Why It Matters
The university information and communication system is often chaotic as deadlines get mixed up, important processes are unclear, and staying informed about campus activities can be difficult. Looped serves as a reliable hub for academic and campus-related updates, helping students stay informed, connected, and actively involved in university life.
